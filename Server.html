Servers

Data stored in the cloud is just as vulnerable. For example, data farms which are large collections of servers that deal with cloud storage require large amounts of electricity to keep cool. Overheating of these servers can result in large amounts of data being lost (Wieringa, 2017).

WIERINGA, M. 2017. Junctions: Graduate Journal of the Humanities. The Fragility of Digital Media Content. Vol 2. (Issue 2.).
